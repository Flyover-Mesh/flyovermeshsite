Our next community node is being built and will be named after **June Bacon-Bercey** (1928-2019), a Wichita native and true pioneer. She was the first African American woman to earn a degree in meteorology (UCLA, 1954), the first Black female broadcast meteorologist, and the first woman to receive the American Meteorological Society's Seal of Approval.
