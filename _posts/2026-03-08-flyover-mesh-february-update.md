## 🛠️ Tools and Coverage

Our coverage tools continue to improve. You can see messages received and node contacts from bots around the city:

- [**MeshMerge Coverage Map**](https://meshmerge.fly.dev/map.html)
- **Frood Bot Map**: [meshtastic.ekpyroticfrood.net](https://meshtastic.ekpyroticfrood.net/)
- **#mesh-map channel on Discord**: Check if your messages are getting through.
- **bot response on the mesh**: fl0v, HYDB, and HYDD are all set up to respond on the mesh to different commands. `$ping` `$coinflip` `$8ball` and `$time` will reply with short messages. While `$weather` and `$quote` will reply with longer messages to test areas for better coverage. Both the command and the reply will show in #mesh-map on Discord to give you a view from both sides.

Turn on your node, send a message, and see how far it goes. This is our weekly chance to test coverage and connect with the community.
Here’s how it works:
- Check in in Discord's #events channel with: your approximate location and node short name “checking in mid-town, k10n”
- Send a message on the default long fast channel on the mesh, something like “mesh net check in”
- Watch for connections on the mesh
- Report contacts you see in the Discord #events channel thread

## 🚀 How to Get Started

1. Check out our [Recommended Devices](https://flyovermesh.org/posts/meshtastic-recomended-devices/) page for options at every budget and experience level. Purchases made through our affiliate links help fund community node hardware and maintenance.
2. Read [How to Join Flyover Mesh](https://flyovermesh.org/posts/how-to-join-meshtastic-community-network/) for setup instructions.
3. Download the Meshtastic app on your phone.
4. Pair your radio and start messaging.

Have questions? Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/5h93pEjTcz) and ask in #advice.

With 175+ members and multiple community nodes across the city, there's never been a better time to join.
